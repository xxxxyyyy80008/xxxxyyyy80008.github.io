<h2> Modeling horse racing in Hong Kong </h2>

<h4> Introduction: </h4>

<p>
Betting on racehorses is one of the most loved activities in Hong Kong. The Hong Kong Jockey Club (HKJC) - the biggest tax payer and monopoly of legal betting on horse racing in Hong Kong - puts on a race meeting on almost every Wednesday and Sunday. 

<p>
The HKJC’s pari-mutuel betting system allows those who bet on horses races to use innovative technologies in predicting the outcomes of each race. HKJC also publishes all racing results on its website.
